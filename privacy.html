Privacy Policy

Your privacy is extremely important to us. Simple Dictionary is designed to respect and protect your personal information.

  • No personal data is ever shared with any third party.
  • We do not use ad targeting, data mining, or any activity that may compromise your privacy.
  • Your word searches and history are stored locally on your device and never sent to our servers.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your data for advertising or marketing purposes.
  • No tracking or analytics are used to monitor your activity in the app.

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy, please contact us at simpledictionary.help@gmail.com.
