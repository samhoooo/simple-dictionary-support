üîÑ How do I cancel my subscription?
To cancel your subscription:
  1. Open Settings on your iPhone/iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Find "Simple Dictionary" and tap it
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"
You'll still have access until your current billing period ends.
üîä Why can't I hear pronunciations?
If pronunciations aren't working:
  1. Check your device volume is turned up
  2. Make sure silent mode is off
  3. Go to Settings > Accessibility > Spoken Content
  4. Turn on "Speak Screen" and "Speak Selection"
  5. Download an English voice under "Voices"
üí≥ How do I restore my purchases?
If you've already purchased premium on this device:
  1. Open the app
  2. Go to the pricing page
  3. Tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom
Your premium features will be restored automatically.
